A layered story where a street hustle becomes raw material, a newsroom turns it into a broadcast, and the hustler watches himself as a character on TV. The intent is to create an echo loop: lived action is observed and documented, then consumed by its originator, hinting that the broadcast will alter his future choices. Two actors perform approximately four distinct action types across alley, office, and home episodes, with refinement adding a confrontation/response layer to complete the loop. The Inception-style complexity emerges from documentation and consumption layers referencing and timing themselves after the lived event, setting up a feedback effect in later stages.
